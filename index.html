About

Focused on digital marketing questions with real behavioral and strategic relevance.

My research studies marketing from a digital perspective, with a focus on new media, online advertising, and consumer behavior in technology-rich environments. I am especially interested in how users respond to digital platforms, virtual settings, and AI-enabled changes in the market.

I combine empirical modeling with behavioral tools such as surveys to answer questions that connect consumer insight with firm strategy. I am seeking a faculty position where I can continue building a research pipeline at the intersection of marketing and digital technology.
